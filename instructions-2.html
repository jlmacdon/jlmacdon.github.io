How to Play

Both players will place one piece at any unoccupied spot on the board each turn. After a piece has been placed on the board, it cannot be moved for the duration of the game. What's unique about this game is that rather than picking your own piece to play, your opponent chooses from the available pieces that you then place on the board.

Here is an example of a complete turn:
1. Your opponent selects a piece for you to put on the board.
2. You place the piece onto one of the available spots on the board.
3. You select a piece for your opponent.
4. Your opponent places the piece onto one of the available spots on the board, then picks a piece to give to you.
