Description

You will be playing this game against a computer. You will place one piece each turn onto a 4x4 grid board. There are sixteen pieces in total.

Each piece has four characteristics:
Color(red OR blue)
Size(big OR small)
Shape(circle OR square)
Hole(hole OR no hole)

Objective

The goal of the game is to line up four pieces on the board that share at least one characteristic.

Pieces can be lined up horizontally (in a row), vertically (in a column), or diagonally.
