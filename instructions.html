Your Objective in the Game

Your goal in the game is to line up four pieces on the board that share one or more characteristics: e.g., four large pieces of any shape lined up horizontally (in any row), vertically (in any column), or on one of the two diagonals.

What makes this game unique and fun is that rather than picking your own piece to play, you or your opponent chooses from the available pieces the one that must then be placed on any unoccupied spot on the board. After a piece has been placed on the board, it cannot be moved for the duration of the game.


Description

You will be playing this game against a computer. You will place one piece each turn onto a 4x4 grid board. There are sixteen pieces in total.

Each piece has four characteristics:
Color(red OR blue)
Size(big OR small)
Shape(circle OR square)
Hole(hole OR no hole)
