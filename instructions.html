In this experiment, you will be playing a problem-solving game called Quarto. Below, we will explain how to play this game.

Your objective is to get four pieces of the same type in a row, either by color (red/blue), shape (square/circle), size (small/large), or detail (hole/no hole) onto a 4x4 grid board. You can win the game by placing the four similar pieces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

You will be playing against the computer, which will pick each piece for you to put on the board. The piece that the computer selects will appear in the top right-hand side of the board. To select the spot where you wish to put your piece, simply click on the area on the board. In turn, you will select a piece for the computer to place on the board. You can do this by clicking on one of the pieces to the left of the board. This will give that piece to the computer.
